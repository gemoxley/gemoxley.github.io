Gray Moxley is a transplant to foggy San Francisco from equally cloud-covered Boston. He credits the lack of Vitamin D growing up with a deep love of staying inside and drawing all day. He is currently attending the University of San Francisco with a double major in Advertising and Design, and definitely drinks a few too many energy drinks.

His skills include endless patience battling the full Adobe suite of products, as well as a mastery of the Procreate suite. Additionally, he’s well versed in HTML and CSS with plans to delve into JavaScript in the future.

In his free time, he enjoys listening to music, doodling, playing video games and doing stand-up comedy. His goal in life is to one day illustrate children’s books, and he’s secretly deeply terrified of horses.
